Idemia Singapore — Full Stack Developer · 2023–2025

Integrated facial recognition for border customs clearance

The Problem

Manual identity verification at border checkpoints was slow, error-prone, and created bottlenecks. The client needed a automated biometric solution that could process travelers in seconds while maintaining high accuracy.

The Solution

Designed and integrated a facial-recognition pipeline connecting biometric cameras, passport readers, and QR scanners into a unified clearance workflow. Built demo software to showcase the integrated system to prospective clients globally.

▸ 20% reduction in manual verification time
